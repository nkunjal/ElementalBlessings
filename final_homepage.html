In the rich, fantasy world the Elemental Blessing novels are set in, people believe that five essential elements rule all things and guide their lives. In this world, people regularly visit temples where they can choose blessings that are supposed to guide an individual through life.

The majority of people in this society associate with an element more than the others, and thus individuals usually get blessings from a specific element more than the others.

Upon the birth of a child, traditionally the father asks three strangers to pick a blessing each for the newborn. These three blessings symbolically illustrate the predominant characteristics that the baby will have later in life.
