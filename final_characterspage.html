Zoe Ardelay

Zoe is a coru woman, although her father and mother are sweela. In fact, she is the coru prime; a woman who can control the deep waters of the mighty river. With the blessing of power, Zoe may destroy the delicate balance of power in the palace.

Darien Serlast

As a hunti man, Darien does not stop until he gets his way. He is a regent of the kingdom and he advises the king on matters of great importance. He keeps many secrets, however at the end of the day, everyone counts on his to keep the kingdom running.

Navarr Ardelay

Navarr is a sweela man, who was known for quick wits and trouble making. In a feud with Christara Lalindar, Zoe's grandmother, he was banished from the palace. Many characters in the novel remember him for his intelligence and passionate personality.

King Vernon

The king has a very childish demeanor, yet he is responsible for an entire kingdom. He has yet to name his successor in the book Troubled Waters, which is a central point of the plot in said book. With his royal advisors close at hand, he takes good care of his people.

Christara Lalindar

Christara was the last coru prime. As the prime, she dealt with the turmoiling palace life, and helped advise the king. She was known for her strong personality, and cleverly steered herself through rough times.

Queen Elidon

Elidon is the king's first queen. She bore Vernon no children, yet still bears the position of the most caring woman amongst the queens. She is an elay woman, and is often chided by the other queens because of her age.

Queen Seterre

As the second queen of Vernon, Seterre is a hunti woman. She has one daughter, Josetta, who is fourteen. She is a schemer, but a cautious one, who would pretend to be someones best friend, all while taking petty measures to make said person unhappy.

Queen Alys

The most ambitious of the four queens is Alys who is the king's third wife. She has one daughter, Corene, and she is determined to have Corene be the next heir to the throne. Alys is a sweela, which is fitting considering her elaborate plots in the palace.

Queen Romelle

The fourth and last queen is Romelle, who is a torz. She, too bore the king one daughter, Natalie, however her daughter is fussy and not very strong. She is the youngest and most likable of the four, and she believes the world likes her back.
